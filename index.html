Financial Logic

Current barriers

Low buyer trust, low financing access, no committed offtaker, and revenue dependence on electricity.

Green Grade intervention

Certification, procurement/offtake, investor disclosure, and MRV strengthen credibility.

Economic outcome

More stable cash flow, lower perceived risk, and improved bankability profile.

Illustrative improvement

IRR 8-10% → 12-15%; payback 12-16 → 8-11 years; DSCR 1.0-1.2x → 1.25-1.5x.

Grade Pathway

Grade A

Eligible for PNRE partnership, offtake negotiation, green finance packaging, and annual re-rating.

Grade B

Transition-ready. Eligible after targeted improvements in low-scoring dimensions.

Grade C

Conditional. Requires corrective action plan before market activation.

Grade D

High-risk or incomplete. Requires redesign and better project evidence.
